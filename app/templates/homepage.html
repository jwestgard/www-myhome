This website is my personal workspace and home of some public research projects. For information about me, see my professional homepage at UMD.

Research Projects

  • Census of Bede Manuscripts: A comprehensive inventory of the medieval manuscripts of the works of the Venerable Bede.
  • Coding projects on Github:
    • csv-validate: Command-line tool for validation of CSV datasets against rules defined in a YAML schema file.
    • preserve.py: Command-line tools for digital preservation tasks, including creating ineventories of files and verifying checksums.
    • fcrepo-serial-restore: Python script to restore the contents of a Fedora 4 repository from its serialized form (RDF plus binaries) stored on disk.
